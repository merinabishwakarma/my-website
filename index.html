Empath • Creator • Caretaker

Hello everyone, welcome to my personal website. I am Merina/Ganga from Nepal. I am a young individual interested in the mysteries of life. My life has taken me to many ups and downs; I come from a low-middle-class family where we have to work constantly to sustain ourselves.

Since my father’s accident, he has been in a coma and bedridden. He needs constant nursing care—feeding, cleaning, and checking. It takes at least two individuals, me and my mom. My brother is in a foreign land working hard to support us. We struggle as any normal family does; we grind to invest in our dreams financially and mentally while finding peace in what we have.

"I am not looking for sympathy, but this is the truth. In this reality, my family finds reasons to wake up, show up, and smile. I find strength here."

I see many people complaining, comparing, and getting frustrated. I am an empath. I stay humble. I believe we will reach where we are supposed to reach. I learned this from my father’s accident. He was the kindest man I ever knew. Life doesn't care who is good or bad, but that doesn't mean you take the bad side. We can all choose to be kind.

In the past, I was more carefree. I learned dancing, won Miss Bishwakarma and Miss Damak 1st Runner-up, and was involved in The Hunate Movement (formerly The Joon Project). But for the last 3 years, I have not left my father's bed. Sometimes I feel I am 'late' in life when I see friends on social media, but I repurpose my thoughts. Life will fix everything if you learn to stay happy and positive.

I started my business, Merry El's, and I am thankful to everyone who helped with my father's treatment. I have also started the RAW TALK podcast with my friend Khem, founder of the Hunate Movement. Khem is a man of immense wisdom who helped me overcome my mental habits and find my purpose. You can learn more from him at khemrajpradhan.com.np.
