Hello everyone, welcome to my personal website. I am Merina/Ganga from Nepal. I am a young individual interested in the mysteries of life. My life has taken me to many ups and downs; I come from a low-middle-class family where we work constantly to sustain ourselves.

Since my father’s accident, he has been in a coma and bedridden. My mother and I provide 24/7 care together. My brother works hard in a foreign land to support us. We struggle as any normal family does; we grind to invest in our dreams while finding peace in what we have.

"I am not looking for sympathy, but this is the truth. In this truth and reality, I together with my family find peace, happiness, reasons to wake up, and reasons to smile."

In the past, I was more carefree. I won Miss Bishwakarma and Miss Damak 1st Runner-up, and was involved in social research with The Hunate Movement. Now, though I am grounded at home, I repurpose my thoughts and stay positive. I believe life will fix everything if you learn to stay happy.

I have started the RAW TALK podcast with my friend Khem, a man of immense wisdom who helped me find my purpose. You can learn more from him at khemrajpradhan.com.np.
